I'm excited to share that my paper [(Agentic Knowledge Distillation: Autonomous Training of Small Language Models for SMS Threat Detection)](/assets/pdf/ai_ss_paper.pdf) has been accepted to the [1st International Workshop on AI Safety and Security (AI-SS 2026)](https://cyber.kent.ac.uk/events/AI-SS2026/), co-located with the [21st European Dependable Computing Conference (EDCC 2026)](https://edcc.dependability.org/). The workshop will take place on **7 April 2026** at the University of Kent, Canterbury, UK.
