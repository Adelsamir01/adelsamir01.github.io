On **7 April 2026**, our paper **["Agentic Knowledge Distillation: Autonomous Training of Small Language Models for SMS Threat Detection"](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.10869)** received the **Best Student Paper Award** at **[AI-SS 2026](https://cyber.kent.ac.uk/events/AI-SS2026/)**, the 1st International Workshop on AI Safety and Security, co-located with **[EDCC 2026](https://edcc.dependability.org/)** at the [University of Kent](https://www.kent.ac.uk/), Canterbury, UK.

The work is co-authored with Joshua Sylvester, Budi Arief, and Rogério de Lemos.

[IEEE UK & Ireland published an event review of AI-SS 2026](https://www.ieee-ukandireland.org/event-review-1st-international-workshop-on-ai-safety-and-security-ai-ss2026/) covering the programme, keynote, panel discussion, and award announcements—including the Best Student Paper.
