I was invited by the [Egypt Red Crescent](https://egyptianrc.org/) Unit to deliver an online session on **Generative AI** for students at [Galala University](https://www.gu.edu.eg/), Egypt.

The session explored how students can take advantage of Gen AI in various ways—from research and learning to productivity and creative applications. I explained key concepts, practical use cases, and answered all questions from the audience.

**50+ students** attended this online session, making for an engaging and interactive discussion.
